Hey everybody. I'm Casey Sharp (pen name of Ciara Smith). I created this site because I really struggle to make time for my own writng. I work with books, so sometimes I feel like if I have energy to write in my free time, I have energy to work. Which isn't how that works lol. I remembered the excitement from the Event That Shall Not Be Named and thought, well I could do that. (Of course, because I have a very ambitious brain). But I also thought November, with Thanksgiving family gatherings was always a difficult month. October is my favorite month, so Samhain Scribing Month was born! I hope you enjoy.(Also please keep in mind that I know a little HTML so, no, the website isn't 100% great, but it's what I can do.)
